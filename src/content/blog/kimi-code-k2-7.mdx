I love underhyped, underground, niche stuff. Instead of sending my money to the Americans, why don't I send it to the Chinese and try their models! Kimi K2.7 is supposed to be quite good after all.

The model is alright, the subscription is not. The $20 US tier gives you less usage than Codex/Claude for worse quality but the same price. Terrible value proposition. Not worth discussion more than that. I cancelled right away.
